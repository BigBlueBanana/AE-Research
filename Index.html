Overview

NBA stands for National Basketball Association. It is the highest level of basketball in the U.S.A. and many talented players from all over the world have the goal of playing in it. It currently has 30 teams, 15 in the Western Conference and 15 in the Eastern Conference, and about 450 players. Each team plays 82 games in a season.



History of the NBA

The NBA was founded on June 6, 1946 by Walter Brown. In the first season, there were 11 teams who competed in a 60 game season. As the league expanded, the number of teams increased and existing teams moved and changed names. One very notable event in the league's history is the merging of the NBA and the ABA (American Basketball Association) in 1976. Throughout the 70's, the ABA was a prominent basketball league that featured players such as Julius Erving, George Gervin, and Rick Barry.



Notable Players

The NBA has featured many extremely talented players, though there are some that most people agree are a step above everyone else. For exaple, Michael Jordan and Lebron James are mostly accepted as the two best players of all time, with most arguments over the best player being about those two. Some other players who are widely accepted as top 10 candidates are Kareem Abdul-Jabbar, Shaquille O'Neal, Kobe Bryant, Magic Johnson, Larry Bird, Bill Russel, Wilt Chamberlain, Hakeem Olajuwon, and Tim Duncan. Currently, some of the best players in the league are Joel Embiid, Nikola Jokic, Jimmy Butler, Stephen Curry, Lebron James, Jayson Tatum, Giannis Antetokounmpo, and Luka Doncic.



Awards

The most popular seasonal awards in the NBA are the MVP, Rookie of the Year, Defensive Player of the Year, Sixth Man of the Year, Most Improved Player,Finals Most Valuable Player, and Coach of the Year. Kareem Abdul-Jabbar has the most MVP's with 6, Dikembe Mutombo and Ben Wallace are tied for most DPOTY with 4 each, Jamal Crawford and Lou Williams are tied for the most Sixth Man of the Years's with 3, Michael Jordan has the most Finals MVP's with 6, and Larry Brown, Don Nelson, Gregg Popovic, and Pat Riley are tied for the most Coach of the Year's with 3 each. In 2023, Joel Embiid got the MVP award, Paolo Banchero got the ROTY award, Jaren Jackson Jr. got the DPOTY award, Malcolm Brogdon got the Sixth Man of the Year award, Lauri Markkanen got the Most Improved Player award, Mike Brown was the Coach of the Year, and the Finals MVP has yet to be decided.



International Affairs

Over the entire history of the NBA, the most common country for players to be from is the U.S.A. However there have been many players from foreign countries, and many players currently in the league are from foreign countries. Canada has the highest number of foreign players with some notable players such as Steve Nash and Shai Gilgeous-Alexander. Along with Canada, some of the most common foreign countries in the NBA are France, Serbia, Australia, and Nigeria. Some current players from foreign countries are Nikola Jokic (Serbia), Joel Embiid (Cameroon), Giannis Antetokounmpo (Greece), and Luka Doncic (Slovenia).
